About The Great Outdoors

Connecting people with nature while preserving it for future generations

Our Mission

We are dedicated to connecting people with the incredible hiking opportunities in the Pacific Northwest while promoting sustainable trail use and environmental stewardship. Our goal is to help hikers discover new trails, stay safe, and minimize their impact on the natural environment.

By highlighting lesser-known trails alongside popular destinations, we aim to disperse visitor impact and preserve the natural beauty of our most beloved locations for generations to come.

What We Stand For

Environmental Stewardship

We promote Leave No Trace principles and encourage responsible hiking practices to protect our natural spaces.

Safety First

We provide comprehensive safety information and resources to help every hiker prepare properly and return home safely.

Community

We build a community of respectful hikers who share trail experiences and help each other discover new adventures.

The Hidden Gems Initiative

Our Hidden Gems section highlights lesser-known trails that offer incredible experiences without the crowds. Popular trails can become overcrowded, leading to trail degradation, parking issues, and diminished experiences for everyone.

By showcasing alternative trails, we help distribute hiking traffic more evenly across the region, reducing environmental impact on heavily-used trails while helping you discover beautiful places you might have otherwise missed.

Support Us

The Great Outdoors is an independent project built with passion for the Pacific Northwest hiking community. Your support helps us maintain the site, add new trail information, and continue providing free resources to help hikers explore safely and responsibly.

All donations go directly toward hosting costs, development, and maintaining accurate trail information.
