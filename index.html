Trail Safety & Leave No Trace

Our trails are a shared treasure. Help protect them for future generations.

Leave No Trace Principles

  • • Pack out all trash, including food scraps
  • • Stay on designated trails to prevent erosion
  • • Keep wildlife wild - no feeding
  • • Respect other visitors and trail etiquette

Spring Season Advisory

  • • Trails may have snow at higher elevations
  • • Check weather and trail conditions before you go
  • • Carry the 10 Essentials on every hike
  • • Share your hiking plan with someone
